## Intro to Web Development

Are you curious about how websites are built? Do you have an idea for an app but don't know where to start?

The **Youth Coding Workshop** is designed for beginners ages 13-18. No prior experience is required!

### Curriculum
1.  **HTML Basics:** Structuring a webpage.
2.  **CSS Styling:** Making it look cool with colors and fonts.
3.  **JavaScript Magic:** Adding interactivity.
4.  **Mini-Project:** Build your own portfolio site by the end of the day!

### Requirements
*   A laptop (If you don't have one, please let us know in the registration form, and we will provide a loaner device).
*   Curiosity and creativity!

### Instructors
The session will be led by senior engineers from top local tech companies who are volunteering their time to mentor the next generation of innovators.
